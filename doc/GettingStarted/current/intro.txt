== Release Notes ==

The Release Notes for the current Pyomo release can be viewed at
https://github.com/Pyomo/pyomo/blob/master/RELEASE.txt

=== Change Log ===

The Pyomo Change Log is distributed with Pyomo and can be viewed at
https://github.com/Pyomo/pyomo/blob/master/CHANGELOG.txt

=== Determining Your Version of Pyomo ===

To determine your current version of Pyomo, use the command
[shell]
----
pyomo --version
----
